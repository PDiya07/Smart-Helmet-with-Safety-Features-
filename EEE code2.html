Smart Helmet - More Details

Implementation Details

The system integrates **GPS, GSM, and an accelerometer** to detect accidents and send alerts. A pressure sensor ensures the helmet is worn.

Technology Used

**Microcontroller:** Arduino/ESP32
**GPS Module:** NEO-6M
**GSM Module:** SIM800L
**Accelerometer:** MPU6050
